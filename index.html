I'm a web developer in Nashville.

My wife, daughter, & I moved here in September, 2015 to work for Dave Ramsey. Prior to that, my development career began unexpectedly soon after college, when I started self-teaching the basics. I'm now excited and grateful that I get to use the skills I love to help advance a mission that matters.

My approach to development prides keeping things light, efficient, and unique. You won't see my projects using an off-the-shelf theme that's tweaked a little bit, or an overbloated drag & drop solution that kills performance and SEO. If we're going to build something, we're going to build it right, performant, and effective.
