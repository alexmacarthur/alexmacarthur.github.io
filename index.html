I'm a web developer in Nashville.

My wife, daughter, & I moved here from Minnesota in September, 2015 to work for Dave Ramsey. Prior to that, my web development career began unexpectedly when I self-taught the basics after college. Since then, I've enjoyed learning as quickly as I can, working on a variety of freelance projects along the way (see just a few below).

My approach to web development prides keeping things light, efficient, and well-organized. You won't see my projects using a standard, off-the-shelf theme that's tweaked a little bit, or an overbloated drag & drop solution that kills performance and SEO. If we're going to build something, we're going to build it right, performant, and effective.
